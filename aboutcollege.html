About College

1. Introduction

College is altogether a new ball game. The freedom, thrills, cafeteria gossips, new friends, new courses, etc. ups the ante to a whole new level.

2. Initiate The Basics

The teens are new to the interview culture. An interview is not about answering all questions asked by the interviewer. It is the opinion of both shared with each other. Professional dress, maintaining eye contact, confident reply, etc. are the plus points. Make your children aware of these points from the start itself.

3. Maintain Proper Dress Code

Most college goers like funky. It works okay in the campus but when it’s come to an interview, it’s not going to work. The right decorum must be maintained. Ask your boy/girl to be in a professional dress and teaming up clothes with sober and subtle colors. Semi-formals can also work.

4. Plan Properly

Proper planning leads to the flawless execution. Prepare a list of the upcoming challenges and tasks that your kid’s going to face.

  1. Marks criteria
  2. Personal Analysis
  3. Extra talent Analysis

5. Explore The Campus

The preparation can be made smooth on the kid’s end if you take a stroll around the campus with your kid showing him/her how things work there. Have a little chat with the coordinators and the professors. Introduce your child to them. It will definitely help in building confidence.

6. Know The Pros and Cons

Talk to the existing students of the college and ask about the pros and cons of the college, the lecturers, safety culture, academic sophistication, social aspects, etc.
